The Artist

About

I'm a visual artist and projection designer working at the intersection of fine art, technology, and live experience. I build things that make people stop and look twice — whether that's a building coming alive with light, a rug that belongs in a museum, or a concert stage that breathes.

My background spans classical painting, 3D animation, interactive installation, and real-time visuals. I've worked across architectural projections, gallery shows, festival stages, and brand activations — translating ideas into immersive moments.

I work out of a fully-equipped studio and bring the full pipeline in-house: concept, design, production, and on-site execution. No handoffs, no middlemen.
